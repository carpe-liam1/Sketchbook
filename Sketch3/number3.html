With this sketch I wanted to give the feeling of being "rushed" so I searched up how to make object gravitate towards the cursor. By giving the ellipse the positioning of x and y and setting these variables to initially there is distance between the cursor and ellipse. Then, to make the ellipse edge towards the cursor, I made it so that if the ellipse's coordinates are less or greater than the coordinates of mouseX and mouseY then it should move up, down, left or right in that direction. By doing this the ball seems to be always following you or "rushing you" to move wherever.
