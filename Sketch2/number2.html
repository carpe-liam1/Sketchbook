For this sketch, I wanted to try to display the adjective cold. Initially, I had planed to try to create a visual with snow (small white ellipses) falling from wherever the cursor would be. That proved to be too compicated so I instead looked up how to display quick changes in positioning which when close together would look like shaking or shivering. I wanted this to occur when the ellipse was outside the house and in the cold blue air. When inside, the ellipse and rectangle/house turn orange signifying the change in tempurature.
