Micro Projects Ideas

Idea 1: Meticulous tasks

Intended Feeling: anxiety

The interaction I hope to make with this one is a feeling of focus as though things could fall through at any point. The plan for this sketch would be to make a maze through rectangles or other shapes. The mouse will be a medium sized ellipse. If the ellipse is to ever touch the "walls" of the maze, the screeen will turn red. Additionally, the input of the mouse touching the wals will add to a score sheet at the bottom of the sketch. I think that the unevenness could come from mouse pressing. when one presses the mouse, the ellipse wil grow to a random size making the manuevering easier or harder.


Idea 2: Meandering growth

Intended Feeling: welcoming

My goal with this sketch would be to emulate the joy of seeing where things go. What I would like is if I made an ellipse that grows from a different angle/point every time the mouse is clicked. The input will be the mouse clicks. The change will be the everchanging shape of the "ellipse" and the unevenness will be in the way by which the ellipse grows.


Idea 3: Close but not quite

Intended Feeling: frustration

The interaction I hope to create with this sketch is a sense of frustration that comes from being close to a goal but not quite reaching it. I imagine an ellipse which will follow the user's mouse wherever it goes but never actually centers on the cursor. The input is the movement of the mouse. The change will be the movement of ellipse while the unevenness can come from the different ways/speeds which the ellipse follows the mouse.
